The Love Between Noto Sans and Fjalla One

An amazing love story

Fjalla One is apart of the Sans Serif and was carefully adjusted to the restrictions of the screen. However, Fjalla One is not very verstile, which is one of its biggest disadvantage. Despite this, Fjalla One has the ability grab a reader's attention with its boldness, which makes it great for headings. Noto Sans is a global font that can be used to write in all modern and anciet lanugages. Noto is also very verstile by having multiple italic styles, weights and widths.

Why Noto Sans and Fjalla One?

These two simply embrace eachother by their differences in weight, scale, and width. Paired together, a reader can clearly see the differences in hierarchy and together, they can used to break down longer text. With Fjalla One being a great for headings and subheadings, with Noto Sans being great for any body of text.
